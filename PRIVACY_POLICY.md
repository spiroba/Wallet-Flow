# Privacy Policy for WalletFlow

**Last Updated: November 27, 2024**

## Introduction

WalletFlow ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our WalletFlow mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

WalletFlow stores all subscription data locally on your device. This includes:
- Subscription names and details
- Payment amounts and dates
- Categories and custom settings
- App preferences (theme, language, notifications)

**This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable iCloud sync.**

### iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud sync:
- Your subscription data is stored in your personal iCloud account
- Data is synced across your Apple devices using your Apple ID
- We do not have access to your iCloud data
- Apple's privacy policy governs iCloud storage

### Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases:
- Transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store
- We do not collect or store your payment information
- Apple's privacy policy governs purchase transactions

### Analytics and Diagnostics

WalletFlow does **NOT** collect:
- Personal identification information
- Usage analytics
- Crash reports sent to our servers
- Location data
- Device identifiers for tracking

## How We Use Your Information

All data processing happens locally on your device:
- Display your subscriptions and payment schedules
- Calculate spending analytics
- Send local notifications for upcoming payments
- Sync data across your devices (if iCloud is enabled)

## Data Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell your personal data
- Share your data with third parties
- Use your data for advertising
- Transfer your data to external servers

## Data Security

Your data is protected by:
- iOS device encryption
- Optional Face ID / Touch ID protection within the app
- Apple's iCloud encryption (if sync is enabled)

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- **Access**: View all your data within the app
- **Delete**: Remove any subscription or delete all data
- **Export**: Your data remains on your device/iCloud
- **Disable Sync**: Turn off iCloud sync at any time in Settings

## Children's Privacy

WalletFlow does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences to track subscriptions.

## Third-Party Services

WalletFlow uses the following Apple services:
- **StoreKit**: For in-app purchases (governed by Apple's privacy policy)
- **CloudKit/iCloud**: For optional data sync (governed by Apple's privacy policy)
- **EventKit**: For optional calendar integration (data stays on device)
- **UserNotifications**: For local payment reminders (no data sent externally)

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

- Шифрованием iOS
- Face ID / Touch ID (опционально)
- Шифрованием Apple iCloud
